BAILMENT AGREEMENT


This BAILMENT AGREEMENT (“Agreement”) is made on this <%= [●] %> day of <%= [●] %>, 2024 (“Execution Date”) BETWEEN:


  1. BKS MyGold Private Limited , a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 with CIN U36911UP2021PTC152605, and having its registered office at 3rd Floor,M-13, Gole Market Mahanagar, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh-226006 (hereinafter referred to as the “Company” , which expression shall, unless it be repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, be deemed to mean and include its successors and permitted assigns) of the FIRST PART
  2. <%= [●] %>, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 with CIN <%= [●] %> , and having its office at <%= [●] %>(hereinafter referred to as the “Bailee”, which expression shall, unless it be repugnant to the context or meaning thereof, be deemed to mean and include its successors and permitted assigns) of the SECOND PART

(Company and the Bailee are hereinafter collectively referred to as the “Parties” and individually each as a “Party ”.)


WHEREAS

  1. Company is engaged in the business of providing the Bailed Gold on bailment basis.
  2. Bailee has approached the Company for facilitation and availing of the bailment of the Bailed Gold and has agreed to pay the Rental for the Bailed Gold in accordance with this Agreement (“Bailment”).
  3. The Parties are now desirous of recording their understanding and have accordingly agreed to enter into and execute this Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the representations, warranties, mutual agreements and covenants contained in this Agreement and other good and valuable consideration (the receipt and adequacy of which are hereby mutually acknowledged), the Parties hereby agree as follows:

  1. DEFINITIONS


    In this Agreement, the following words and expressions shall, except where the context otherwise requires, have the following meanings respectively:


    “Bailed Gold” means the pure 24 karat gold, which will be granted to the Bailee in terms of this Agreement;


    “Gold Drawdown Notice” shall mean the form annexed with this Agreement as Schedule, which shall be executed by the Company and the Bailee, specifying inter alia, the Bailed Gold, Rental, Rental Payment Date, and the applicable Repayment Date;


    “Rental” shall mean the rent payable to the Company by the Bailee, for the Bailed Gold for a Gold Drawdown Notice, as agreed at the time of the Bailment;


    “Rental Payment Date” means the date on which the Bailee is required to pay the rental on the Bailed Gold, in physical gold of 24 karat purity or such other form as specified by the Company, in form and substance acceptable to the Company; and


    “Repayment Date” shall mean the date on which the Bailee shall return the entire amount of the applicable Bailed Gold, as specified in the Gold Drawdown Notice, as per terms and conditions of this Agreement.

  2. INTERPRETATION


    In this Agreement:

    1. he recitals and schedules shall constitute an integral and operative part of this Agreement;
    2. all references to agreements, documents or other instruments include (subject to all relevant approvals) a reference to that agreement, document or instrument as amended, supplemented, substituted, novated or assigned from time to time;
    3. unless the context otherwise requires, the singular includes the plural and vice versa;
    4. the words ‘hereof’, ‘herein’, and ‘hereto’ and words of similar import when used with reference to a specific Clause in, or Schedule to, this Agreement shall refer to such Clause in, or Schedule to, this Agreement, and when used otherwise than in connection with specific Clauses or Schedules, shall refer to the Agreement as a whole;
    5. headings and the use of bold typeface shall be ignored in its construction;
    6. a reference to a Clause or Schedule is, unless indicated to the contrary, a reference to a clause or schedule to this Agreement;
    7. references to this Agreement shall be construed as references also to any separate or independent stipulation or agreement contained in it;
    8. the words “other”, “or otherwise” and “whatsoever” shall not be construed ejusdem generis or be construed as any limitation upon the generality of any preceding words or matters specifically referred to;
    9. references to the word “includes” or “including” are to be construed without limitation;
    10. words importing a particular gender include all genders;
    11. references to any law shall include references to such law as it may, after the date of this Agreement, from time to time be amended, supplemented or re-enacted;
    12. words and abbreviations, which have, well known technical or trade/commercial meanings are used in the Agreement in accordance with such meanings; and
    13. the rule of construction, if any, that a contract should be interpreted against the parties responsible for the drafting and preparation thereof, shall not apply.

  3. ROLE OF THE COMPANY

    1. Notwithstanding anything contained in this Agreement, the Company shall be entitled to take any such actions as it may deem fit to protect the Bailed Gold. Any determination by the Company in relation to the quantity, purity or other matters relating to the Bailed Gold or the Rental shall be binding on the Bailee.
    2. Any decision to provide the Bailed Gold to the Bailee shall be at the sole discretion of the Company and the Company reserves the right to not provide the Bailee with any Bailed Gold. At the time of any Bailment of any Bailed Gold, the Company and the Bailee shall execute the Gold Drawdown Notice, specifying the terms of the Bailment of the Bailed Gold. It is clarified that the Parties may execute one or more Gold Drawdown Notices during the term of this Agreement for the Bailment of the Bailed Gold from time to time. The Bailed Gold shall be provided as per the terms specified in the Gold Drawdown Notice and which terms shall form a part of this Agreement. The Bailed Gold shall be delivered to the Bailee, as specified in the Gold Drawdown Notice.

  4. TERMS OF BAILMENT

    1. In consideration of the Bailment of the Bailed Gold, the Bailee shall pay to the Company, the applicable Rental on the applicable Rental Payment Dates. Rental, whether calculated on a daily or monthly basis, will be payable daily or monthly and will be computed using a basis of 365 (three hundred and sixty-five) days a year and the actual number of days elapsed. The Company’s determination regarding the payment and the receipt of the Rental shall be final and binding on the Bailee.
    2. The Bailee further agrees to pay any fees and charges as specified by the Company from time to time for the Bailment.
    3. The Bailee shall be entirely liable in relation to the Bailed Gold and shall be responsible to safeguard the same. The Company shall be entitled to inspect the storage and safeguard requirements for the Bailed Gold, and the Bailee shall take measures to safeguard the Bailed Gold as specified by the Company from time to time. In case of any loss, damage, pilferage of the Bailed Gold, the Bailee shall be entirely responsible for the same and shall be required to entirely compensate the Company for any such loss, damage, pilferage or destruction. The Bailee shall also be responsible for obtaining insurance for the Bailed Gold, as specified by the Company.
    4. Subject to the Gold Drawdown Notice, title to, and ownership of the Bailed Gold shall not be transferred to the Bailee and the Bailee is merely receiving the same for the purposes as specified in this Agreement and a Gold Drawdown Notice.
    5. Bailee agrees that each Bailment in relation to Bailed Gold shall be completed only after successful delivery of Bailed Gold to the Company on the Repayment Date (or earlier in case of termination of this Agreement), in form and substance acceptable to the Company. The Bailee further agrees that in case of Bailee’s default of returning the Bailed Gold or the failure to pay any Rental, the Company shall be entitled to take all measures to recover such Bailed Gold any unpaid Rental.
    6. Without prejudice to Clause 6 (Termination and consequences), in case of any change in applicable laws, the Company reserves the right to modify this Agreement and the arrangements contemplated herein for the effective performance of this Agreement.
    7. The Company is not obligated to disclose to the Bailee or provide any information to the Bailee to the origin and source of the Bailed Gold or the terms of underlying contract entered into between the Company and its customers or any other information relating to the transaction entered into between the Company and its customers.

  5. SECURITY

    1. The Bailee agrees that at the time of the Bailment in terms of any Gold Drawdown Notice, the Bailee shall deposit, in the bank opened and maintained by the Company of its choice, an amount as specified in the Gold Drawdown Notice of the currency value of the Bailed Gold as on the date of such Gold Drawdown Notice, as specified by the Company (“Security Deposit”).
    2. The Bailee shall take all steps and necessary action to create a right of lien of the Security Deposit in the favour of the Company and shall not deal with the same in any manner whatsoever.
    3. The Parties agreed that after successful and satisfactory consummation of the Bailment of any Bailed Gold and payment of all applicable Rentals, the Security Deposit made by the Bailee shall be returned back within 15 (fifteen) days for the return of all the applicable Bailed Gold and the payment of the entire amount of the Rental. The Company may appropriate all or any part the Security Deposit in case of a breach of the terms of this Agreement by the Bailee and the Bailee shall be obligated to top-up such appropriated amount, within 5 (five) days of the appropriation.
    4. In case of any additional security deposit, the Company shall inform the Bailee in writing from time to time, which shall be honoured by Bailee.

  6. TERMINATION AND CONSEQUENCES

    1. The Agreement shall be valid from the Execution Date unless terminated by the Company. The Company may terminate this Agreement with a prior written notice of 30 (thirty) days without assigning any reason whatsoever.

    2. Otherwise as provided in Clause 6.1, the Company reserves the right to terminate the Agreement for the following reasons:

      1. Upon Bailee’s entry in RBI’s list of defaulters,
      2. Upon Bailee’s failure to provide the Security Deposit in accordance with this Agreement;
      3. Upon Bailee’s failure to pay any Rental on the Rental Payment Date or return the Bailed Gold on the Repayment Date;
      4. Upon Bailee committing any breach of the terms of this Agreement;
      5. Upon Bailee being under or part of any investigation relating to including but not limited to financial fraud, embezzlement and money laundering.
      6. Upon Bailee declaring insolvency or being rendered incapable of paying its debts.
      7. Upon the creditors of Bailee presenting by petition, application and other proceeding against him.
      8. Upon Bailee being charged with crimes including but not limited to crimes relating financial fraud, embezzlement or money laundering.
    3. In the event of where this Agreement is terminated by the Company due to any reasons mentioned herein, (a) the Bailee shall (i) in case of termination pursuant to Clause 6.1 above, on the applicable Repayment Date, and (ii) in case of termination pursuant to Clause 6.2 above, within 3 (three) days of termination, provide the Bailed Gold to the Company, (b) the Bailee shall (i) in case of termination pursuant to Clause 6.1 above, on the applicable Rental Payment Dates till the return of the Bailed Gold, and (ii) in case of termination pursuant to Clause 6.2 above, within 3 (three) days of termination, pay the unpaid Rental, (b) the Company will return the Security Deposit in accordance with Clause 5 (Security), subject to any adjustments.

  7. INDEMNIFICATION


    The Bailee shall indemnify, defend and hold harmless the Company and its directors, shareholders, contractors, employees, staff, agents, consultants and advisers (“ Indemnified Parties”) in full against all costs, expenses, damages and losses, including any interest, fines, legal and other professional fees and expenses awarded against or incurred or paid by the Indemnified Parties, arising out of, or in connection with:

    1. the breach of the obligations, covenants, representations and warranties of the Bailee under this Agreement;
    2. any loss, damage, destruction or pilferage of the Bailed Gold;
    3. any claims by any persons or third-parties in relation to the Bailed Gold; and
    4. any claims by any governmental authorities in relation to the Bailed Gold.

  8. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES


    The Bailee represents and warrants that (which representations will be deemed repeated on each date till all the Bailed Gold is returned to the Company):

    1. The Bailee free to enter into this Agreement without violation of any agreement or restrictive condition that it may have with any third party;
    2. The Bailee is not party to any other arrangement that shall compromise its abilities to carry out its duties;
    3. The Bailee shall not enter into any arrangement which would impose any obligations inconsistent with any terms of the Agreement or which would restrict or conflict with the performance of its obligations under this Agreement;
    4. All the information and documents provided by the Bailee is accurate, complete and updated;
    5. The Bailee conducts its business in compliance with the applicable laws and has not received any notice or other communication from any court, tribunal, arbitrator, government agencies and regulatory body;
    6. It has never, in the last 3 (three) years from the Execution Date, appeared on the RBI’s list of defaulters;
    7. It is not currently under or part of any investigation relating to including but not limited to financial fraud, embezzlement and money laundering;
    8. It is not insolvent or incapable of paying its debts and none of its creditors has presented by petition, application and other proceeding against it for any administration order; and
    9. No order has been passed, no petition or application has been presented, no resolution has been passed for the purpose of winding up/insolvency whereby its assets are to be distributed to the creditors or shareholders.

  9. APPLICABLE LAW AND ARBITRATION


    This Agreement shall be governed by and construed and interpreted in accordance with the laws of Republic of India. The Parties shall use their best endeavour to settle amicably amongst themselves any and all disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement or the interpretation thereof. Sole arbitrator appointed by the mutual consent of the Parties shall settle any outstanding dispute in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996. The venue and seat for arbitration shall be New Delhi and the language shall be English. The costs of the arbitration proceedings shall be borne equally by the Parties. However, each Party shall bear its own expenses in prosecuting or defending its claim.

    1.     2. Notice: Any notices or other communications required or permitted hereunder shall be deemed to have been duly given within 1 (one) business day by the Company when sent on the email address provided by Bailee at the time of signing up on the Platform.
    3. Complete Agreement: This Agreement supersedes any prior agreements, arrangements, understandings, correspondence, memorandum and undertakings between the Parties in relation to its subject matter and together with Annexures constitutes the entire Agreement between the Parties relating to the subject matter.
    4. Modifications and Amendments: No supplement, amendment, or modification of this Agreement shall be binding unless it is in writing and signed by authorized representative of both the Parties.
    5. Severability: If any part or provision of this Agreement shall be held unlawful, rendered void or unenforceable, that provision shall be severed, and the remainder of this Agreement shall remain in effect.
    6. Waiver: The failure of any Party to enforce or exercise, at any time or for any period, any term of or any right arising pursuant to this Agreement does not constitute, and shall not be construed as, a waiver of such term or right and shall in no way affect that Party's right to later enforce or exercise it. The waiver by either Party of the breach of any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of the breach of any other provision or of the subsequent breach of the same or any other provision.
    7. Assignment: The Company shall be free to assign, directly or indirectly, all or part of its rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the Bailee or with a notice to the Bailee.
