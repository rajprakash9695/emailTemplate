REFUND POLICY



This refund policy applies to any transactions which have been initiated by a customer (“Customer”) on the electronic platform (“Platform”) provided by BKS MyGold Private Limited (“Company”).


Please be advised once a transaction has been completed on the Platform, Requests for returns and refunds will only be considered if the seller permits returns, as specified on the product page. Refunds will only be issued under the case of damage, different items or similar case scenarios.


In the unlikely event that a transaction fails and money is debited, the amount will be refunded to the source account.


In the rare instance of an order being lost in transit, the Company allows a timeframe of 15-30 days before considering the order lost. If the order is located successfully, the Company will proceed with delivery to the given address. However, if the order remains untraceable, the Company will process a refund using the original payment method.


Deliveries are exclusively made to the registered address of the customer. Customers are kindly requested to have proof of identity ready at the time of delivery for verification by the courier partner.


Customers must inspect the contents of their order upon delivery to ensure it has not been tampered with and meets acceptable quality standards. The Company will not entertain any requests claiming damages or defects after delivery.
