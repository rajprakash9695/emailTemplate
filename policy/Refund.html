REFUND POLICY



This refund policy applies to any transactions which have been initiated by a customer (“Customer”) on the electronic platform (“Platform”) provided by BKS MyGold Private Limited (“Company”).


Please be advised once a transaction has been completed on the Platform, no request for any returns and refunds would be entertained. Refunds will only be issued under the following circumstances, upon the customer's request via phone call, email, or through the order panel, subject to approval of the Company:


  1. Cancellation requests made by the customer before the completion of the transaction or before the Company has begun to process the transaction.
  2. Cancellation requests made by the customer in case of incorrect or wrongful provision of goods or services.

In the unlikely event that a transaction fails and money is debited, please contact us at []. Upon receiving such a request, the Company will verify and initiate a refund if the payment has been received for the order. Customers can expect a refund within 7 to 10 working days.


In the rare instance of an order being lost in transit, the Company allows a timeframe of 10 days before considering the order lost. If the order is located successfully, the Company will proceed with delivery to the given address. However, if the order remains untraceable, the Company will process a refund using the original payment method.


Deliveries are exclusively made to the registered address of the customer. Customers are kindly requested to have proof of identity ready at the time of delivery for verification by the courier partner.


Customers must inspect the contents of their order upon delivery to ensure it has not been tampered with and meets acceptable quality standards. The Company will not entertain any requests claiming damages or defects after delivery.
